ADHD App — Visual Directions

Four distinct visual identities for a wellness app that helps people with ADHD track habits, moods, and insights through voice-first interaction.

Best viewed on mobile or with browser DevTools in responsive mode (390px wide)
1
Cozy Illustrated World
Studio Ghibli meets Animal Crossing
A warm, storybook landscape where each feature is a location to visit. CSS-illustrated cottage, rolling hills, and wooden signpost navigation create an inviting, low-pressure world.

Underwater Discovery
Abzu meets bioluminescent deep sea
A vertical ocean cross-section with depth zones. Floating particles, glowing coral mood reefs, and a pulsing voice orb create an immersive, calming dark-mode experience.

Playful Dashboard

Collectible Art Piece
